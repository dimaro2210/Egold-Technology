About Us

Who We Are

Egold Technology was formed in 2002 as a subsidiary of Oria Usifo Consult and incorporated in 2018 by renowned Electrical and IT professionals in Nigeria. Our principal is an Electrical and Computer Engineer, System Integrator and Project Manager with extensive knowledge and experience in Engineering Practice, Renewable Energy Systems, Security and ICT.

Internationally trained in Surveillance Security System and Access control system (ADI- South Africa), we bring world-class expertise to every project we undertake.

Our Services

Our Vision & Objective

We envision a future where technology and engineering solutions create safer, more efficient, and sustainable environments for businesses and communities.

Our primary objective is to provide Engineering services, ICT and security services to organizations and companies in the efficient application of Engineering Technology. Our key activities comprise the supply, design and installation of all types of Engineering services, Renewable Energy Systems, ICT, security systems/accessories and networking.

Roadblocker Security Systems

Our high-security roadblocker systems provide ultimate protection for sensitive areas, government buildings, corporate headquarters, and other high-risk locations. These heavy-duty barriers are designed to stop unauthorized vehicle access while maintaining smooth traffic flow for authorized vehicles.

Our roadblocker solutions include:

  • Hydraulic and electro-mechanical road blockers
  • Crash-rated barriers for high-security applications
  • Automatic license plate recognition integration
  • Remote control and monitoring systems
  • Emergency override features
  • Custom designs for specific site requirements

Our roadblocker systems are engineered to withstand extreme conditions and provide reliable operation 24/7. We conduct thorough site assessments to determine the optimal placement and configuration for maximum security effectiveness.
