About Us

We strive to provide high-quality services with a customer-first approach. Our dedication to innovation and excellence sets us apart. At Egold Techonology, We are a passionate team dedicated to delivering high-quality products and services. Egold Technology was formed in 2002 as a subsidiary of Oria Usifo Consult and incorporated in 2018 by renowned Electrical and IT professionals in Nigeria. Egold Technology Principal is an Electrical and Computer Engineer, System Integrator and a Project Manager with wide knowledge and Experience in the Engineering Practice, Renewable Energy Systems, Security and ICT. Internationally trained in Surveillance Security System and Access control system (ADI- South Africa).

Our Vision & Objective

We envision a future where our services empower individuals and businesses to achieve their full potential through cutting-edge solutions.
Our primary objective is to provide Engineering services, ICT and security services to organizations and companies in the efficient application of Engineering Technology. Egold Technology key activities comprise the supply, design and installation of all types of Engineering services, Renewable Energy Systems, ICT, security systems/ accessories and networking. which include; Project Management, Solar Powered System‐ design and installations, Security System, System Integration, Access Control system, Wireless networks, Structured Cabling and UPSinstallation etc.
