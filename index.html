About Us

Engineering Excellence Since 2002

Egold Technology was formed in 2002 as a subsidiary of Oria Usifo Consult and incorporated in 2018 by renowned Electrical and IT professionals in Nigeria. Our principal is an Electrical and Computer Engineer, System Integrator and Project Manager with extensive knowledge in Engineering Practice, Renewable Energy Systems, Security and ICT.

Internationally trained in Surveillance Security Systems and Access Control (ADI — South Africa), we bring world-class expertise to every project.

Our Vision & Objective

We envision a future where technology and engineering solutions create safer, more efficient, and sustainable environments for businesses and communities.
